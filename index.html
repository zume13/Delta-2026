ABOUT ME

Passionate about building technology that matters.

I'm a full-stack .NET Software Developer with hands-on experience delivering real-world solutions using C#, ASP.NET Core, TypeScript, and Microsoft SQL Server. I have a strong passion for clean architecture and building scalable systems that are secure, maintainable, and efficient.
